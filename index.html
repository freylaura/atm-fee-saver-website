Do you travel interantionally to a foreign country and pay lots of fees to local ATMs, just to withdraw your own money?

ATM Fee Saver mobile app helps you find local ATMs in a foreign country that charge the lowest fees* to withdraw cash,
when you use your non-local debit card.

*ATM Fee Saver provides info on “ATM usage fees” — Fee of a local ATM to provide cash withdrawal service, to a foreign or overseas debit card holder.
a.k.a ATM usage fee, ATM operator fee, ATM service fee, ATM transaction fee, etc. The app also provides info on ATMs’ transaction / withdrawal limits.

The app does not provide information on potential charges by the card holder’s issuing bank, currency conversion charges, etc.
